VENTWALL - MAJOR VISION UPDATE

This is not Facebook, Reddit, X, Threads, or TikTok.

VentWall is the world's anonymous diary.

The goal is to create a place where people can say what they are really thinking without chasing followers, likes, popularity, or social status.

VISUAL STYLE

Use the attached image as the primary visual inspiration.

The site should feel like:

* A dark late-night coffee shop
* An old typewriter
* A forgotten journal
* A wall of drifting thoughts
* Reading strangers' minds at 2 AM

Dark theme by default.

Use warm amber, parchment, brass, faded paper, charcoal black, and subtle orange highlights.

The page should feel alive.

Add:

* Floating dust particles
* Tiny drifting paper fragments
* Faint floating text in the background
* Slow ambient movement
* Soft glowing effects
* Occasional paper flutter animations

Everything should move slowly and naturally.

The site should feel like breathing, not flashing.

LANDING PAGE

The moment someone arrives they should immediately see the wall.

Do NOT hide the feed behind menus.

The feed is the product.

Show real posts drifting upward immediately.

Top section:

VENTWALL

"The World's Anonymous Diary"

or

"Say It. Drop It. Let It Go."

Show:

"147 voices drifting through the wall"

Main buttons:

🔥 Vent Anonymously

🕯 Join VentWall

Anonymous should be the primary action.

LIVE WALL

The feed should feel like a river of human thoughts.

Posts continuously drift upward.

New posts appear with a subtle typewriter animation.

Examples:

"I lost my job today."

"My wife beat cancer."

"I don't know what I'm doing anymore."

Posts should appear as parchment paper cards.

Cards should feel handwritten or typed.

TYPEWRITER INPUT SYSTEM

The bottom of the screen is VentWall's signature feature.

Instead of a normal text box:

Display a vintage-inspired typewriter.

As users type:

* Keys animate
* Paper moves upward
* Cursor blinks
* Typewriter sound plays if enabled

Post button:

THROW IT INTO THE VOID

When submitted:

The paper leaves the typewriter.

Floats upward.

Joins the live wall.

This animation should feel magical.

SAFETY SYSTEM

VentWall is for expression.

Do NOT over-moderate.

Allowed:

* Profanity
* Anger
* Political opinions
* Religious opinions
* Ugly emotions
* Dark humor
* Emotional venting

Blocked:

* Credible threats
* Doxxing
* Child exploitation
* Criminal instructions
* Terrorist content
* Spam and scams

SELF-HARM SAFEGUARD

If AI detects possible self-harm:

Show:

"You sound like you may be going through something heavy right now."

Display:

* Call/Text 988
* Crisis Resources
* Continue Posting

Do not automatically delete unless necessary.

THREAT SAFEGUARD

If user threatens another person:

Block post.

Show:

"You may vent anger. Direct threats are not allowed."

POST LIFESPAN
