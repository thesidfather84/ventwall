VENTWALL – SAFETY, MODERATION, LEGAL PROTECTION, TERMS OF SERVICE, AND PRIVACY UPDATE

Objective:
VentWall is intended to be a free-expression platform where users can anonymously share thoughts, frustrations, celebrations, opinions, and life experiences. The platform is not intended to host illegal content, threats, harassment, or dangerous activity.

=========================
AUTOMATED MODERATION
====================

Implement a layered moderation system:

Level 1: Free Automated Filters

* Keyword filtering
* Pattern matching
* Spam detection
* Link abuse detection
* Personal information detection
* Phone number detection
* Email detection
* Address detection

Level 2: Flagged Content Review
Automatically flag content involving:

* Suicide
* Self-harm
* Violence
* Murder
* Terrorism
* School threats
* Child exploitation
* Doxxing
* Revenge porn
* Illegal activity instructions
* Credible threats

Level 3: Community Reporting
Add:

* Report button on every post
* Block button
* Mute button
* Hide post button

Auto-hide content after multiple reports until reviewed.

=========================
CRISIS RESPONSE
===============

If content appears related to suicide or self-harm:

Display:

"If you are in immediate danger or thinking about harming yourself, contact local emergency services or call/text 988 in the United States."

Do not publish obvious self-harm encouragement.

=========================
CONTENT NOT ALLOWED
===================

Users may not post:

* Credible threats
* Terrorist content
* Child sexual content
* Doxxing
* Stolen personal information
* Revenge pornography
* Fraud schemes
* Illegal weapon manufacturing instructions
* Malware distribution
* Human trafficking content
* Content prohibited by applicable law

VentWall supports anonymous speech.

Users may:

* Vent frustrations

* Share unpopular opinions
