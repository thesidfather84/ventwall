Build a mobile-first web app called VentWall.

Concept:
VentWall is an anonymous text-based live wall where people can vent, confess, celebrate, rant, or say what they need to get off their chest. It should feel like TikTok/Facebook scrolling energy, but without video. It is a live rolling feed of human thoughts.

Landing screen:
When someone opens the site, do NOT send them straight into posting. Show a clean welcome screen with:

VentWall
“Say it. Drop it. Let it go.”

Two large buttons:
1. Join VentWall
2. Vent Anonymously

Also include small links:
- What is VentWall?
- Safety Rules
- Terms of Service
- Privacy
- Settings

Design:
Make it look like a real app, not a plain website.
Dark mode by default.
Modern mobile-first layout.
Feed should feel alive.
Use a typewriter / rolling wall effect where new vents appear with a subtle typing animation.
The wall should look like a stream of floating text cards.
Add soft movement so the feed feels active, but not annoying.

Main feed:
Users see a live endless scrolling wall.
Each post card should show:
- Emotion tag
- Text post
- Anonymous or username
- Time left before post expires
- Echo reactions
- Reply button
- Report button

Post expiration:
Anonymous vents stay live for 7 days.
Profile/user posts stay live for 30 days.
Old posts should automatically expire and disappear from the feed.

Posting:
User can post as:
- Anonymous
- Username/profile

Post button text:
“Throw It Into The Void”

Emotion categories:
- Angry
- Sad
- Happy
- Stressed
- Lonely
- Celebration
- Random Thought
- Confession
- Need Advice
- I’m Okay Now

Reaction system:
Do NOT use Facebook-style likes or thumbs up.

Use “Echoes” instead.

Echo reaction buttons:
- Heard
- I Feel That
- Same Wave
- Not Alone
- Let It Burn
- Keep Going
- Needed This

Show reaction counts like:
“42 Heard”
“18 Same Wave”
“9 Not Alone”

Anonymous mode:
A person should be able to use the site without making an account.
They can click “Vent Anonymously,” type a vent, pick emotion, and post.

Join mode:
Users can create a simple username and profile.
Profile should be optional.
No real name required.
Profile users can save their posts and keep them longer.

Safety and AI moderation:
This is a venting platform, so do NOT over-moderate normal anger, profanity, politics, religion, ugly thoughts, or offensive language.

Allowed:
- anger
- cussing
- controversial opinions
- emotional vents
- dark humor
- rough language
- people saying they are upset, lost, angry, lonely, or tired

Blocked or escalated:
- credible self-harm intent
- credible threat to hurt someone else
- doxxing
- addresses or phone numbers posted to attack someone
- child sexual content
- instructions for crime
- direct calls for violence
- spam, scams, bots
- targeted harassment campaigns

Self-harm safeguard:
If a post sounds like the person may hurt themselves, show a popup before posting:

“You sound like you may be in a really heavy place right now. VentWall is here for expression, but if you may hurt yourself, please contact help now.”

Add buttons:
- Call or text 988
- Crisis resources
- Continue posting

Serious threat safeguard:
If a post says they are going to hurt or kill a specific person, block the post and show:

“We can’t publish threats to harm another person. You can vent your anger, but direct threats are not allowed.”

What is VentWall page:
Explain:
VentWall is a live anonymous wall of thoughts. It is not about followers, popularity, or looking perfect. It is a place to get something off your chest and see that other people feel things too.

Safety Rules page:
Short version:
1. Venting is allowed.
2. Profanity and ugly emotions are allowed.
3. Threats are not allowed.
4. Doxxing is not allowed.
5. Exploiting minors is never allowed.
6. Spam and scams are not allowed.
7. You are responsible for what you post.
8. VentWall may remove content that creates real-world danger.

Terms of Service starter:
Create a Terms page with simple readable sections:
- Acceptance of Terms
- Anonymous Posting
- User Responsibility
- Content Rules
- No Emergency Service
- Crisis Resources
- Moderation Rights
- Post Expiration
- No Guarantee of Privacy
- Account Termination
- Contact

Important TOS language:
VentWall is not therapy, medical care, legal advice, crisis counseling, or emergency support. If someone is in immediate danger, they should call emergency services or 988.

Database:
Create tables/models for:
- users
- posts
- reactions
- comments
- reports
- moderation_logs

Admin dashboard:
Create a simple admin dashboard where admins can:
- view reported posts
- remove posts
- see moderation flags
- search posts
- ban abusive users or device/session IDs if needed

Tech:
Use React frontend.
Use a clean backend/API.
Use database storage.
Make it mobile responsive.
Make it deployable as a website first.
Add comments in the code so a beginner can understand it.

Final goal:
A working MVP website where someone opens VentWall, sees Join or Vent Anonymously, posts a vent, and watches it appear on a live rolling wall with Echo reactions and basic safety moderation.
